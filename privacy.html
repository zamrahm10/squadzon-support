Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

SquadZon ("we", "our", or "us") operates the SquadZon mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

  • Account Information: Username, display name, email address.
  • Profile Information: Bio, playstyle, preferences, and other profile details you choose to provide.
  • User Content: Messages, clan chats, and other content you create within the app.
  • Usage Data: Interaction with matchmaking, lobby features, and app functionality.
  • Purchase Information: Subscription and in-app purchase data processed via RevenueCat and Apple.
  • Push Notification Data: Device push tokens to deliver notifications.

How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain app functionality
  • Enable matchmaking and communication features
  • Authenticate users and prevent fraud
  • Process in-app purchases
  • Improve performance and user experience
  • Provide customer support

Third-Party Services

We use third-party services that may collect information in accordance with their own privacy policies:

  • Expo – Push notifications
  • RevenueCat – In-app purchase management
  • Apple App Store – Payment processing

We do not sell your personal data and do not use your information for third-party advertising.

Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information. However, no system is completely secure.

Data Retention

We retain user data as long as your account remains active or as necessary to provide services and comply with legal obligations.
