SquadZon – Account Deletion Request

How to Delete Your SquadZon Account

You can permanently delete your SquadZon account directly within the app:

  1. Open the SquadZon app
  2. Go to Profile → Settings
  3. Tap Delete Account
  4. Confirm the deletion request

If you are unable to access the app, you may request deletion by contacting us at:

gaminetmail@gmail.com

Please include your registered email address or username in the request.

What Data Is Deleted

When your account is deleted, we permanently remove:

  • Profile information (username, email, avatar)
  • Chat messages
  • Friends and clan memberships
  • Game coin balances
  • Premium status
  • Stored preferences

What Data May Be Retained

Some data may be retained where required for legal, tax, fraud prevention, or security purposes, including:

  • Transaction records related to in-app purchases
  • Fraud prevention and abuse logs
  • Limited system logs necessary for compliance

Retained data is stored only for the period required by applicable law and then securely deleted.

Processing Time

Account deletion requests are processed within a reasonable timeframe. Once deleted, your account cannot be restored.
