SquadZon Safety Standards (Child Safety & CSAE)

SquadZon is committed to protecting minors and preventing child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE). We have zero tolerance for any content, behavior, or activity involving sexual exploitation of minors.

Prohibited Content and Behavior

The following are strictly prohibited on SquadZon:

  • Any sexual content involving minors, including content that sexualizes or exploits minors
  • Child sexual abuse material (CSAM) in any form
  • Grooming, solicitation, or attempts to contact minors for sexual purposes
  • Requests for sexual images or sexual conversations with minors
  • Content that promotes, depicts, or facilitates sexual exploitation of minors

Enforcement

We investigate reports and take action that may include content removal, account restriction, and permanent bans. When appropriate, we may preserve relevant information for legal compliance and cooperate with law enforcement.
